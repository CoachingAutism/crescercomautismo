About me

Melanie Mata
Born on 7 October 1978.

I am a psychologist graduated from Universidade Lusófona (2008) and certified in Understanding Autism (Milton Keynes College, UK). I am also a coach specialized in autism.

I have worked with children in Mozambique (After School), with adults in the United Kingdom, and developed a deep passion for autism. Today I offer online coaching with individualized strategies for families and also work directly with children on the spectrum here in the UK.

My vision for the future
My dream is to apply my experience in less favoured places, where autism support is still limited.

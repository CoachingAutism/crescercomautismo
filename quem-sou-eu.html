Who am I

Melanie Mata
Born on 7 October 1978

I’m a psychologist, graduated from Universidade Lusófona (2008), and I hold a certificate in Understanding Autism (Milton Keynes College, UK). I am also a Coach specialized in autism.

I have worked with children in Mozambique (After School), with adults in the United Kingdom, and that’s where I developed my passion for autism.

Today I offer online support with individualized strategies for families, and I also work directly with children on the spectrum here in the UK.

My Vision for the Future
My dream is to apply my experience in less privileged places, where support for autism is still limited.
